This course will be in-person. Students are expected to read papers ahead of each course session, and discussions will be moderated to ensure critical thinking and in-depth understanding. The course is offered for 6 credit units and 12 credit units; students enrolled for 12 credit units will be expected to complete a comprehensive research project during the semester.

The complete syllabus for Spring 2026 is currently being finalized. Please check back later for updates.

For reference, you can view the [Spring 2023 syllabus](https://cmu-multicomp-lab.github.io/asi-course/spring2023/syllabus/).
