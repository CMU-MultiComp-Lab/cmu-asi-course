This course will be in-person. Students are expected to read papers ahead of each course session, and discussions will be moderated to ensure critical thinking and in-depth understanding. The course is offered for 6 credit units and 12 credit units; students enrolled for 12 credit units will complete a comprehensive research project during the semester.

The complete syllabus for Spring 2026 is available on Canvas. Please email the instructors if you have any questions.
